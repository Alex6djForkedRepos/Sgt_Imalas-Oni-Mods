[h1]Canned Food[/h1]

This mod adds a canned barbeque and canned tuna, non spoiling food variants that are made at the crafting station.

[h1]Current latest Version: 2.1.0[/h1]

[hr][/hr]

[h1]Features[/h1]
New Foods: Canned Barbeque & Canned Tuna
[list][*] made at the crafting station.
[*] Canned BBQ has 2000kCal/kg, Canned Tuna has 800kCal/kg.
[*] requires 500g of (refined) Copper OR Aluminium (configurable in Mod Settings)
[*] does not spoil
[*] designed for space missions!
[*] once eaten, can scraps are dropped, those can be recycled back into the metal used on construction
[/list]

[h1]DLC Features[/h1]
[list][*] Canned Tuna will provide Radiation Resistance, similar to normal sea food.
[/list]
