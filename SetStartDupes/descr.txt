[h1]Introduction[/h1]

This mod allows to adjust the amount of Duplicants you start the game with.
Also features individual selection of traits, interests and reactions of duplicants.
Allows to customize the look of a dupe independent of its stats.


[h1]Current latest Version: 3.1.0[/h1]

[hr][/hr]
[h1]Features[/h1]
[list]
[*] By Default, enabling the mod sets the amount of Duplicants to start with to 3.
[*] This value is adjustable in mod settings to start with a number of between 1 and 100 duplicants.
[*] Allows to individually change the different Traits, Interests and Reactions of a Duplicant (similar to DGSM).
[*] Has option to spawn with additional food and oxylite for every additional start dupe
[*] Option to reenable rerolling for printing pod dupes
[*] Allows customize the looks of a dupe independent of its skills. (doesn't work for sussy dupes from bio inks)
[/list] 

Compatible with [url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=2869608898]Bio-Inks[/url] 
Compatible with [url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=1717999057]Always 3 Interests[/url] 
Incompatible with DGSM

[h1]Known Issues[/h1]
[list]
[*]Similar to mods done before, having fever than 3 duplicants prevents the Game-Start-Popup to become visible. Hit Esc. to close the invisible start message popup.
[/list]
