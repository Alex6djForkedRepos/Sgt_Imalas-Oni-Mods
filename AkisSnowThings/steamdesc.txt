[h1]Decor Pack S - Winters Delight[/h1]

adds 4 new winter themed decor buildings and a new tree plant to decorate your base with

[hr][/hr]
[h1]Features[/h1]
[list][*] Snow Machine (Decorative): A decorative building that produces falling snow particles
[*] Snow Sculpture: a new type of sculpture made from snow
[*] Glass Case: A new utility building that can be used to vacuum seal snow and ice sculptures to prevent them from melting
[*] Evergreen Tree: A new plant that either produces wood or tree sap. Its seeds can be found in a printing pod care package or produced in the molecular forge. A fully grown evergreen tree can be upgraded into a:
[*] Festive Tree: This building attaches to fully grown evergreen trees to give a festive atmosphere. Once a year it also offers a special surprise for your duplicants..
[/list]
