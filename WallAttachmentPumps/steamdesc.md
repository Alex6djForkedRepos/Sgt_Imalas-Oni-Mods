[h1]Wall Attachment Pumps[/h1]

This mod adds 2 new pumps (gas + liquid) that have to be constructed attached to a wall, pumping from the other side when powered.

[img]https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Sgt-Imalas/Sgt_Imalas-Oni-Mods/refs/heads/master/Compat_All.png[/img]

[h1]Current latest Version: 1.0[/h1]

[hr][/hr]

[h1]Features[/h1]
[list][*] Two new pump buildings, Gas and Liquid Wall Attachment Pump
[*] Size; 1x1, must be constructed attached to a tile
[*] Cost: 50 refined metal, 1 gasket
[*] Unlocked by: Valve Miniaturization tech
[*] Power draw and pump speed are half of a regular vanilla pump
[/list]
