[h1]Extra Trait: Bioluminescent[/h1]

Adds a new trait to the game that makes duplicants glow in the dark.

[h1]Features[/h1]
[list][*] New Trait: Bioluminescent
[*] Trait is available in all game versions
[*] Same Rarity as "Glowstick", use [url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=2856555858]DSS[/Url] to easily add it to new dupes
[*] Glow color of both traits (Bioluminescent and Glowstick) can be adjusted in the dupe sidescreen
[*] New Consumable: Biolumin Essence - crafted at the molecular forge from a shinebug egg and a vaccilator recharge; allows adding the trait to new dupes
[/list]
